Narendra Damodardas Modi (Gujarati born 17 September 1950) is an
Indian politician who has served as the 14th prime minister of India since May 2014.
Modi was the Chief Minister of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014
and is the Member of Parliament (MP) for Varanasi.
